NYC Mobile Companion

Welcome! This map was created to provide visitors and tourists a guide to essential needs while they are out and about!

Need a restroom? Water fountain? Phone-charging station? Or need a place to relax after a long day touring the city?

Use the top-left Search bar to go to a specific location. Use the bottom-left icons to go to your current location and navigate the map. On the map, Click the colored dots and colored plots for more info.

Colors correspond to the following services. Click the boxes and circles below to filter services on the map.
Parks
Pedestrian Plazas
Privately Owned Public Spaces (POPS)
Public Restrooms
Street Seats
Benches
Water Fountains
LinkNYC Kiosks (Wi-Fi, charging, etc.)

Feel free to check out the Source Code and Data. Or contact Chloe Zheng at chloez@nyu.edu with any comments or concerns.
