NYC Mobile Companion

Welcome! The goal of this map is to provide vistors and tourists a guide to essential needs while they are out and about!

Need a restroom? Water fountain? Phone-charging station? Or perhaps after a long day touring the city, you want to find somewhere to sit and relax outside?

Use the Search bar at the top left of the map to find the closest services to a specific location. Use the Services section below to identify specific services. Click the colored dots and colored plots on the map for more info.

The colors below correspond to the following services. Click the boxes and circles below to filter services on the map.
Parks
Pedestrian Plazas
Privately Owned Public Spaces (POPS)
Public Restrooms
Street Seats
Benches
Water Fountains
LinkNYC Kiosks (Wi-Fi, charging, etc.)

Feel free to check out the Source Code and Data. Or contact Chloe Zheng at chloez@nyu.edu with any comments or concerns.
